What have I been up to, lately?

Updated as of December 2, 2025.

Living in Toronto (ON) in Canada, as of early July.

Working full-time at Google in the Developer Relations team in Google Cloud. Just passed my 5th anniversary!

Spent the past few months more on the road than at home. For the most part, work opportunities brought me to the 4 corners of North America, covering 11 cities in 11 provinces and states.

Not working quite as much on XIV ToDo. I hope to find more time soon to get back at it. I’ve also been working on-and-off on a spec-compliant IRC client that I hope to “finish” by the end of the year.

Picked up learning Japanese again, after a long hiatus. I’m taking the JLPT N4 in mid-December.
